# Recommended KPIs - Business View

This page defines **business-level Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)** aligned to the **Contextual / Business View** of the SABSA framework.

These KPIs are used by executive leadership to:
- Validate security investment outcomes
- Track alignment between security and business objectives
- Support risk-informed decision making

---

## Business-Aligned Security KPIs

| Category | Metric | Purpose |
|--------|--------|---------|
| **Product First Development** | % of releases passing all security gates on first attempt | Demonstrates maturity of integrated DevSecOps and secure-by-design delivery. |
| **Safeguard Data** | % of classified assets encrypted in line with policy | Validates data-protection coverage and compliance with classification requirements. |
| **Enhance Experience** | MFA success vs. abandonment ratio | Ensures strong authentication without degrading user experience. |
| **Cloud-First Enablement** | CSPM compliance score | Evidences consistent security governance across cloud environments. |
| **Build Resilience** | Annual disaster recovery exercise success rate | Demonstrates continuity, recovery readiness, and operational resilience. |
| **Optimize Cost** | OPEX savings achieved through security automation | Quantifies efficiency gains and return on security investment. |

---

## Governance Notes

- These KPIs are **outcome-oriented**, not tool-specific.
- Metrics should be:
  - Automatically collected where possible
  - Reviewed regularly in executive governance forums
  - Used to inform investment, prioritisation, and risk acceptance decisions
- KPI definitions and thresholds may evolve as business objectives and risk appetite change.

---

## Traceability

These KPIs support and are traceable to:

- Contextual Business Drivers (BD-01 through BD-06)
- Conceptual Security Principles
- Management reporting and performance dashboards

Metrics without business traceability are considered informational only.
